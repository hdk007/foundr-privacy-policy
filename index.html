Privacy Policy

Last Updated:

Transparency is our priority at CoFoundr AI.

1. Introduction

Welcome to CoFoundr AI. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the startup ideas, research queries, and personal data you share with our AI companion. This policy explains how we handle your data.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with high-quality brainstorming and research, we process the following:

  • User Inputs: The text prompts, startup ideas, and questions you type into the chat.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics (e.g., app crashes, feature usage) to improve performance.
  • Device Info: Minimal device identifiers required for app functionality.
3. AI Processing & Third Parties

This application utilizes Large Language Models (LLMs) to generate responses.

Google Gemini API: Your chat inputs are sent to Google's servers for processing. We do not sell your startup ideas to third parties, but data processed by the API is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Data Retention

We believe in ephemeral brainstorming. We do not store your chat history permanently on our servers. Your chats are stored locally on your device. Once you clear your app data or delete the app, your brainstorming sessions are removed from your device.

If you have questions about this policy or want to request data deletion, please contact our support team:
